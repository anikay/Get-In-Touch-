Titan Core
by Velaura AI

Titan Core is Velaura AI's flagship silicon program — purpose-built for the demands of robotics, physical AI, and next-generation autonomous systems.

We're building the foundational compute architecture that will power the physical world. Titan Core integrates high-efficiency neural processing, real-time sensor fusion, and ultra-low-latency actuation pipelines into a unified SoC platform designed to operate at the edge, in motion, and under constraint.

At Velaura, we believe the next frontier of AI is not in the cloud — it's in the machine. Titan Core is our bet on that future.
