Titan Core™

Velaura AI, Inc. is a leader in ultra-low power compute solutions for data center, edge, and physical AI applications. Power is becoming the critical bottleneck for AI infrastructure at scale. Velaura's patented technology, Titan Core™, helps AI compute platforms deliver dramatically higher power efficiency.

Titan Core™ enables up to 2X lower overall chip power, with potential savings of up to 500W per XPU and substantial reductions in energy, cooling, and infrastructure costs at hyperscale.

Built on a production-proven foundation with tens of millions of ASICs deployed with world-class yield and reliability, Titan Core™ integrates seamlessly into existing architectures while delivering meaningful improvements in total cost of ownership.
